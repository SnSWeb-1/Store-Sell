Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Welcome to the Store & Sell FAQ. Whether you're buying or selling, you'll find answers to our most commonly asked questions below.

Buyers

How do I know the part is in good condition?
Every item is inspected and assigned a verified condition rating. Listings have clear high-resolution photos and notes on any wear.

Can I return a part if it doesn’t fit, or I change my mind?
We accept returns within 7 days if:

  • The item is not as described
  • It was damaged during delivery
  • Incorrect labeling caused a mismatch

We do not accept returns for "change of mind," so read listings carefully!

How long does delivery take?

  • Standard: 1–3 business days
  • Express: Next-day delivery if ordered before 2PM

Can I request a part that isn’t listed?
Yes! Our "Wants List" feature is coming soon to allow part requests.

Is payment secure?
Absolutely. We use encrypted payment systems — you can pay via cards, Apple Pay, or COD.

Sellers

How do I sell a part with Store & Sell?
Simple:

  • Ship or drop your part to us
  • We inspect, rate, and list it
  • You get paid once it sells

Do I need to take photos or write descriptions?
No — we handle all the listings professionally.

What parts can I sell?
Mechanical, cosmetic, performance, and tech parts in good condition — no fluids or oversized engines.

How much commission do you take?
5% transaction fee + optional services (e.g., featured listings or extended storage).

Can I cancel a listing?
Yes — as long as it hasn't sold. We can also ship your part back for a courier fee if needed.

General

Where are you based?
We are based in the UAE, serving all seven Emirates.

Do you have a mobile app?
Not yet — but it’s in the works!

Can garages or businesses sell in bulk?
Definitely. and we’ll get you set up.
