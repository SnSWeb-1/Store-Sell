About Store & Sell

Our Mission

At Store & Sell, our mission is to simplify the way car parts are bought and sold. We empower everyday sellers — from seasoned gearheads to casual car owners — to turn unused car parts into profit, without the hassle of storage, delivery, or complicated logistics. For buyers, we offer a trusted platform where quality, transparency, and convenience come first.

What We Do

  • For Sellers: You ship your car parts to us. We store them securely, rate their condition, photograph them professionally, and list them on our platform. Once the item sells, we pack it and ship it — all you do is get paid.
  • For Buyers: We offer a streamlined, trusted experience with verified quality ratings, clean images, and secure delivery. No ghost sellers. No "used once" turning out to be “used forever.”
  • For the UAE Automotive Community: Whether you're a performance garage, a parts reseller, or a DIYer, we’re the platform that brings structure, safety, and speed to car part sales.

Why We’re Different

  • End-to-End Fulfillment: Most platforms make you do the legwork. We handle inspection, storage, photography, and delivery.
  • Condition Ratings: Every item is rated by us using a consistent 5-point system.
  • Free Storage (for a period): We store your item while it’s listed — so you don't have to cram a gearbox next to your washing machine.
  • Focus on UAE Market: We're built from the ground up with regional logistics, buyer behavior, and seller needs in mind.

Storing With Us

Why Storage Matters

When it comes to selling car parts, storage is often the biggest obstacle. Most sellers don’t have the space to keep bulky or fragile items lying around. Garages are full. Closets are overflowing. And let’s be honest — shipping something like a gearbox from your home every time you get a buyer isn’t exactly efficient.

That’s where Store & Sell changes the game. We don’t just list your item — we store it for you. Securely. Cleanly. Ready to sell.

How It Works

Here’s how our storage system keeps things simple:

  • Step 1: You send the part.
    Once you decide to sell with us, you ship the item to our facility or drop it off if you’re nearby. We’ll provide guidance on packaging and labels if needed.
  • Step 2: We receive & log it.
    As soon as your part arrives, we:
    • Inspect it for condition and usability
    • Assign a unique ID for internal tracking
    • Clean and prep it if required
  • Step 3: We store it.
    Your item is safely stored in a climate-conscious, organized warehouse — away from dust, damage, or theft. Every part is shelved with a QR-coded tag linked to your seller account.
  • Step 4: We list it for you.
    Our team photographs the part professionally and uploads the listing with a detailed description and a verified condition rating (out of 5).
  • Step 5: When it sells, we handle delivery.
    You don’t lift a finger. We package the part, arrange delivery with a trusted courier, and notify you once the sale is complete.

What You Can Store

We currently accept:

  • Mechanical parts (alternators, radiators, gearboxes, etc.)
  • Performance parts (brakes, exhausts, suspension kits)
  • Cosmetic parts (lights, grilles, spoilers, etc.)
  • Audio and tech (head units, sensors, speakers)

Note: We don’t currently accept fluids, oversized engines, or tires with excessive wear. Contact us if unsure.

Security & Care

  • All items are handled with gloves during intake
  • Our facility is locked, monitored, and insured
  • Each part is labeled, shelved, and protected from environmental damage

Why It’s Worth It

  • Save space. No more dusty parts on your porch.
  • Sell faster. Listings with verified ratings and pro photos sell more quickly.
  • Stay worry-free. We manage the storage, condition, and shipment from start to finish.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Welcome to the Store & Sell FAQ. Whether you're buying or selling, you'll find answers to our most commonly asked questions below.

Buyers

How do I know the part is in good condition?
Every item is physically inspected and assigned a verified condition rating out of 5 before it’s listed. You’ll see high-resolution images and a clear summary of wear or damage (if any) right in the listing.

Can I return a part if it doesn’t fit, or I change my mind?
We accept returns within 7 days if:

  • The item is not as described
  • It was damaged during delivery
  • It’s incompatible due to incorrect labeling

We do not accept returns for buyer remorse or incorrect orders, so please read the listing details carefully before purchasing.

How long does delivery take?

  • Standard: 1–3 business days
  • Express: Next-day delivery if ordered before 2PM
  • You’ll receive tracking updates via SMS and email as soon as your part ships.

Can I request a part that isn’t listed?
Yes! We’re launching our “Wants List” feature soon. You’ll be able to post specific part requests, and we’ll notify you when a matching item is listed.

Is payment secure?
100%. We use encrypted checkout systems and don’t store your payment information. You can pay with credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, or cash on delivery (COD).

Sellers

How do I sell a part with Store & Sell?
It’s easy:

  • You ship the part to us (or drop it off)
  • We inspect, rate, photograph, and list it
  • Once it sells, we handle packaging and delivery
  • You get paid — no hassle

Do I need to take photos or write descriptions?
Nope. We handle all listing content for you — including professional photography and condition grading.

What kind of parts can I sell?
We accept most mechanical, performance, cosmetic, and tech parts that are:

  • In working condition
  • Not fluid-based
  • Not overly large

How much commission do you take?
We charge a small transaction fee on every sale (5%) plus optional fees for:

  • Featured listings
  • Extended storage

Can I cancel a listing or get my part back?
Yes, as long as the part hasn’t sold. If you’d like your part returned, we’ll arrange shipping or a pickup — a courier fee may apply.

General

Where are you based?
We operate out of the UAE and currently serve buyers and sellers throughout all seven Emirates.

Do you have a mobile app?
Not yet — but it’s coming!

Can businesses or garages sell in bulk?
Absolutely. We're happy to work with resellers, garages, and auto shops. Reach out to us via to get started.
