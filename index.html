About Us

Store & Sell is a platform for hassle-free automotive part sales. Sellers ship items to us, we store, rate, and deliver them — all you do is list. Buyers benefit from trusted quality and fast service.

We bridge the gap between car part suppliers and automotive enthusiasts. By handling storage and logistics, we make it easier than ever to turn spare parts into value. Whether you're a casual seller or an auto shop offloading inventory, Store & Sell gives your items visibility, credibility, and reach.

Founded in 2025 in the UAE, our mission is to simplify the aftermarket car parts experience through transparency, technology, and trust. We're continuously evolving to serve a growing community of car lovers, professionals, and resellers.

Our team combines industry experience with a deep passion for cars. Every part is inspected, rated, and handled with care to guarantee buyer satisfaction. We also work with garages, custom shops, and enthusiasts to expand our marketplace's offerings and create a connected ecosystem of automotive commerce.
